A List of possible sound effects that can be done thru software. 3 of these will be done in total cuz anymore r kind of just overkill



\# --Distortion--

Hard Clipping (like threshold stuff)

Soft Clipping (smooth peaks)

Applying a Gain and Soft Clipping (tanh(gain \* x))

Fuzz (Square waves basically)

Wave Shaping (make a more complex function to make some weird outputs)

Digital distortion (bitcrush/reduce bits to make a more "compressed" output)



\# Delay

Apply a small delay on the actual output of the audio signal 

Also can do some stuff with echoing



\# Misc.

Do some reverb stuff, seems a lil overkill tho
